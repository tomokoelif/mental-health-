Who We Are

Zen Dojo is a community dedicated to the practice and teaching of Zen Buddhism. Our team consists of experienced monks and practitioners who have dedicated their lives to the study and practice of Zen. We offer a variety of programs and services to help individuals find peace, clarity, and purpose in their lives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a space for individuals to explore and deepen their understanding of Zen Buddhism. We aim to create a supportive community where people can come together to practice, learn, and grow. Through our programs and services, we strive to help individuals cultivate mindfulness, compassion, and wisdom in their daily lives.

Our Team

Shintarou Abe

After graduating from Sojiji Temple, the head temple of the Soto sect of Buddhism, he obtained a Master of Arts from the Graduate School of Aichi Gakuin University. He is a unique monk who embodies the idea of ​​being "neither a monk nor a layman" as he has four faces: a monk, a businessman, a therapist, and a karate practitioner.

Yuji Tamaki

A psychiatrist who graduated from the Faculty of Medicine at the University of Tokyo. He took a leave of absence from his medical career to train at Hasedera Temple, a branch temple of the head temple Eiheiji. He then served as the deputy chief priest and studied Zen and mental stability for many years. He opened a Zen dojo.

Yuriko Koike

After working as a banker, she went to the United States. After working as a radio personality in the United States, she began working as a Buddhist monk. After returning to Japan, she started holding "copying sutras" and "sermons" at Zen Dojo, which attract many worshippers.
